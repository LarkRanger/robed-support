Instructions to remove your data from Robed

We're sorry to see you go. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from within the app.

  1. Open the Robed app on your device.
  2. Make sure you are logged into the account you wish to delete.
  3. Navigate to the Settings menu.
  4. Tap Delete Account at the bottom of the screen.
  5. Confirm the deletion in the prompt that appears.
This action is permanent. All saved items, outfits, and personal data will be irrevocably deleted from our servers immediately upon confirmation.

Individual Data Deletion: You can also request that specific data (such as individual clothing items or generated looks) be removed without deleting your entire account — either by deleting items directly in the app, or by contacting us below.
